Placing An Order

You can now place your order with us via our order form! When you send us an email, we will reach out to you within three business days regarding payment for your order. Once paid, your order will go out ASAP. If you prefer more direct communication, please feel free to reach out to me via my Contact section.

Guidelines for Easier Ordering:

  • Ensure all information is entered correctly.
  • Follow the example text in the description area.
  • Respond to follow up email regarding payment promptly for quickest service.
  • Allow for three business days to receive tracking information after payment.
  • If there is any issue with your order or the website please reach out!
