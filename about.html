MY STORY

What Makes Chefcants Unique

Moses “Chef Moe” Hernandez is a trained chef with over 20 years of food service experience. Although always an avid fan of fragrance, it wasn’t until the Pandemic of 2020 when he began his fragrance collecting journey. The collection grew fast and led to the birth of what is now Chefcants By Chef Moe. A fragrance sampling company geared towards helping anyone find scents they love without spending money on full bottles!
